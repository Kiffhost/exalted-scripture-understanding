*Stamping Inkwarden to the ground, the Lunar creates a binding ward of sigils.*
The Lunar waives Argent-Guardian Yantra’s anima cost (Lunars, p. 204) and extends its duration to indefinite.
It doesn’t end if she moves outside of or through the ward.
