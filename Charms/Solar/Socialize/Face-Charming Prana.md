The Solar bares her soul to another, inviting him to read her intentions. This Charm is a special Socializebased persuade action made as if it were exploiting a Defining Intimacy. Success causes the target to attempt a read intentions action on the Solar. Resisting this influence in a Decision Point costs the target two Willpower.
