*The Solar’s mastery of [[Essence]] flows allows her to guide the movements of pure spirit force.*
This Charm enhances [[Essence-Lending Method]] and [[Will-Bolstering Method]] permanently, allowing both [[Charms]] to be used on targets at short range, without having to touch them. This Charm also lowers the cost of its prerequisite to eight motes.
