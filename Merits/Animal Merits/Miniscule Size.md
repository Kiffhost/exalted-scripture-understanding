This animal receives +3 [[Defense|Evasion]] against larger foes’ [[Attack|attacks]].
Larger characters subtract three successes from [[Awareness]]-based rolls against them.
Against foes with the [[Tiny Creature]] Merit, this drops to +2 [[Defense|Evasion]] and −2 successes.
