*The gods have much to answer for. No more shall their reckoning be postponed.*

The Infernal can attack a dematerialized enemy. She still suffers the usual −3 penalty if she can’t see her enemy.

While using [[Wayward Divinity Oversight]], an attack that 5+ withering damage or any decisive damage to a dematerialized foe renders him visible to others as a translucent apparition for the rest of the scene.
