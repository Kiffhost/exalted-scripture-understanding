Seeing in all directions, Eyebright’s wearer dances untouched through the hurricane’s eye. She ignores (Insight) points of onslaught penalty against the attacks of enemies into which she has Insight. Battle groups into which she has Insight don’t add Size to attack or damage rolls against her.
