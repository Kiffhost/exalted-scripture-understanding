Once, there was a maiden…
...who was born, and told her Mom, “I know how
I’m gonna die.”
“It’s gonna hurt bad,
and I ain’t coming back.”
“...I could avoid it, but I won’t.”
“‘Cause one day, death’d come to me and say,
‘Baby, don’t you know?’”
“‘There’s always an ending.’”
