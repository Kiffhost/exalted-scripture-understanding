Invoking the Chimera’s Coils is a strange Charm. Monstrous transformations, divine auras, the potential threat of unwanted enlightenment—this type of thing is the high-water mark for how strange martial arts can get. Other styles might feature powerful capstones that reach similar levels of esoteric effects and bizarre powers, but those are exceptions rather than the norm.
