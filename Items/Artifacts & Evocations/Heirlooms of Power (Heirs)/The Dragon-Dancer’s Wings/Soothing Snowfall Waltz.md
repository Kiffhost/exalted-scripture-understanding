*Shimmering snowflakes fall from the air and chime with soft melodic tones as the wielder moves with languorous, captivating grace.*
She makes a dance-based ([[Appearance]] + [[Performance]]) inspire roll with (higher of [[Essence]] or 3) bonus dice to calm her audience. The pleasant reverie inspired by this dance grants an additional +1 [[Resolve]] against influence that would inspire or exploit strong emotions, but imposes a −2 penalty on nonreflexive physical actions and on influence rolls that express the influencer’s emotions.
If a Dragon-Blood uses [[Falling Leaves Sway]], [[Swaying Boughs Arabesque]], or [[Waltz of Honeyed Wine]] (Dragon-Blooded, pp. 241-242) with this Evocation, such uses are Air aspected.
