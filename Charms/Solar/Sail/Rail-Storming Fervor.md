*Capitalizing on a perfect moment to strike, the Solar rallies her crew into a precision assault.*
This Charm supplements a [[Join Battle]] roll, adding three bonus dice to the [[Join Battle]] rolls of herself and her crew. Bonus dice from this Charm do not count as dice added by a Charm. [[Rail-Storming Fervor]] is expressly allowed to be combined with [[Join Battle]]-enhancing [[Charms]] of other abilities.
