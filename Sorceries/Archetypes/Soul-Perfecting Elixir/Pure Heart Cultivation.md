The humors governed by the element of fire find their seat in the heart, animating the body and bridging the boundary between flesh and mind as blood flows from the heart to the brain. Once per day, when her Resolve is overcome by influence that opposes an Intimacy based on temperance or abstinence, she may lower the Willpower cost to resist it by one point.
