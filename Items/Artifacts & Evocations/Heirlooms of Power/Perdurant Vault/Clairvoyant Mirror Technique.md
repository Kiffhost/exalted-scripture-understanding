Perdurant Vault’s gleaming surface reflects the hearts of those who engage with its wielder, providing her with secret insight. When the wielder resists an instill action, she makes a reflexive read intentions roll against the influencing character, doubling 9s. Success reveals that character’s [[Intimacy]] most relevant to his attempted influence. For instance, if a House Cynis blackmailer failed to persuade the wielder to aid in a conspiracy, [[Perdurant Vault]] might reveal the Cynis’ Major Tie of hatred for House Cathak that motivates the conspiracy. 

## Special activation rules

This Evocation awakens at no cost when attuned by a Dragon-Blooded Ragara scion.
