*The sorcerer may use [[Occult]] or [[Bureaucracy]] instead of [[Lore]] to [[Introducing a Fact|introduce facts]] about the [[Celestial Bureaucracy]] or the nature and history of a specific god.*
She doesn’t need a [[Lore]] background. If successful, her player can speculate a about a related fact. The Storyteller states whether the speculation’s completely accurate, mostly accurate, somewhat accurate, or completely inaccurate.
