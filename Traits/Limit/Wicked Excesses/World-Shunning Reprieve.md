The world can wait a while as the Infernal partakes in pleasures long overdue. She indulges in all manner of hedonistic revelry and carnal pursuits, enjoying life to the fullest at the expense of any other concerns. No amount of urgency or impassioned appeals can pull her away from her debauchery.

**Duration**: Scene.
World-Shunning Reprieve can be ended prematurely if the Infernal is attacked or if she witnesses the subject of one of her Major or Defining Ties being incapacitated.
