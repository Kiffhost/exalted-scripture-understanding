BurningBranch’sblazingbattlespiritcannotbecontained, erupting in an overwhelming blaze. This Evocation may supplement a decisive attack made on the same turn the Exalt ignites Burning Branch, adding (Essence) bonus successes on the attack roll and doubling 10s on damage.

As long as the wielder isn’t dissonant with jade, this Evocation awakens at no cost when the wielder sockets a fire-aspected hearthstone in Burning Branch.
