*As the Solar works with greater wonders, she achieves a sublime, renewing focus.*
On each interval of a superior roll, when the Solar rolls more successes than the price to finish (typically ten—see “finishing rolls” on p. 240), she earns silver points equal to the rating of the [[Artifact]] under construction plus her current [[Essence]] score.
