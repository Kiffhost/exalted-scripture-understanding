*Having never understood how others can endure the ordeals of being perceived, Tulat eagerly shrouds his wearer from the world.* Tulat’s wearer reduces the cost of [[Charms]] that render her invisible or otherwise imperceptible to any sense or that erase or suppress memories of her by two motes. Sidereals also add +1 to the difficulty of rolls against their [[arcane fate]].
