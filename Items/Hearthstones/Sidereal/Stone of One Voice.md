*Glimmering lines and arcs dance through this milk-blue stone.*
All humans within four miles of the stone find they possess the ability to speak and understand Old Realm, even if they lack the [[Lore]] or [[Occult]] prerequisite. This understanding is unconscious and automatic, though those who make the effort can learn the language permanently with time, as can children raised there.
