The necromancer’s blood is a potent ritual tool. The dead crave it, and it can be leveraged as if it was a Major [[Intimacy]] in bargains. A single [[Health Track|Health Level]] counts as two levels for the purpose of paying costs when using necromancy, and she treats her [[Health Track#wound penalties|wound penalty]] as one lower than it is for the benefit of magic that requires her to spill her blood (e.g., a –1 penalty would count as –2).
