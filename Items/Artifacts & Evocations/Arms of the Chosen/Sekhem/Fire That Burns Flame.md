Sekhem’s fires scorch that which cannot be burned. This attack ignores four points of soak or Hardness, and ignores effects that reduce raw damage from heat or flame. Fire elementals and similar fiery spirits take aggravated damage from the attack. 

## Resonant

This Evocation’s cost is reduced by Sekhem’s (Heat/2, round up).
