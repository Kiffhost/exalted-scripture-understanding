*When all hope is lost, there is still one who can save you.*

The Infernal can use this Charm when she treats a patient facing a grievous ailment — one that’s life-threatening, that could have life-altering consequences, or something similarly weighty. She waives the cost of all Medicine Charms that she uses on a single action.
## Reset
Once per scene.
