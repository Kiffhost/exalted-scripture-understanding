Ghosts trapped in soulsteel are not lucidly conscious but are present enough to suffer in their imprisonment. Bloodshed and necromantic rituals heighten this agony, but a wielder who feels compassion for the doomed dead might slowly learn to soothe the souls bound in her panoply, granting them a brief respite as she wields them.
