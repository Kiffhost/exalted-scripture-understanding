The Sidereal answers the needs and desires of her audience with unfaltering grace and ingenious improvisation. The Sidereal’s Appearance adds bonus dice on an inspire roll to create happiness or other positive emotions. If she succeeds, each affected target’s player must also weaken one of their Intimacies based on negative emotions by one step.
