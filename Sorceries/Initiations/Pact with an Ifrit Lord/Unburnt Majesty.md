*Having contracted with a lord of flame, the sorcerer commands the fealty of lesser fires.*
She doubles 8s on rolls to resist [[Environmental Hazards]] based on fire or heat, and adds her [[Essence]] to both [[Soak]] and [[Hardness]] against purely flame-based attacks such as [[Firewand]] blasts.
