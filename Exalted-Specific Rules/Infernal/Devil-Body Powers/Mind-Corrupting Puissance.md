The Infernal’s curse seeps into her victim’s mind as a Psyche effect, condemning him to never feel hope, burning away his memories in pale fire, dooming him to forever love her, etc. The Infernal chooses an appropriate (Attribute + Ability) pool appropriate to the effect, and a specific curse she wishes to inflict. The otherwise functions like [[Body-Warping Blight (Torment)|Body-Warping Blight]].
