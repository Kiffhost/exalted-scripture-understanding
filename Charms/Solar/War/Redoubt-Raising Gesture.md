The Lawgiver knows the path to victory is through efficiency and planning. With less time for preparation, and fewer tools, she can quickly turn a battlefield into an abattoir. By supplementing a Strategic Maneuver roll with [[Redoubt-Raising Gesture]], the Solar can complete a specific stratagem with one less success than is required.
