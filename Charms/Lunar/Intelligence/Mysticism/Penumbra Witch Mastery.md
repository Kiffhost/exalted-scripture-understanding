*A student of Creation’s mysteries and those that lie beyond the world, the Lunar’s insight illumines uncanny darkness.*
She adds (higher of [[Essence]] or 3) dice and rerolls 1s until they cease to appear on a [[Templates/sorcery]]- or thaumaturgy-related roll; a roll to understand or [[Introducing a Fact|introduce a fact]] about a supernatural being, artifact, manse, or similar phenomenon; or a read intentions, [[Profile Character]], or [[Case Scene]] roll opposing a spirit or fae.
