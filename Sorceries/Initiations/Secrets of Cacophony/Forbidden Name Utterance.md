Once per scene, the sorcerer can speak a secret name that holds the attention of a great being as part of a Shape Sorcery action, stealing power as she invites its ire. She gains (Essence + Charisma) additional sorcerous motes, but is wracked by a backlash that deals one level of unavoidable aggravated damage.
