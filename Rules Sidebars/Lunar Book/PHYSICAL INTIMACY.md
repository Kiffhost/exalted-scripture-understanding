Charms that refer to physical [[intimacy]] encompass any form of intimate contact, such as a passionate embrace or kiss, in addition to sex. It need not be romantic in nature; platonic expressions of affection count. The Storyteller should “fade to black” if any player at the table feels uncomfortable with depicting explicit [[intimacy]] “on screen.”
