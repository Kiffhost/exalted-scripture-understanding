These large, fast ships serve as pleasure barges for the very rich, but many are converted into cargo transports or swift pirate vessels, valued for their speed and stowage.
# Role
Pleasure craft; personal or official blue-water transport; small pirate corsair
# Cargo
Three well-appointed cabins for six passengers, plus dining and entertaining facilities. Pirates pack in many more crew in much less comfort.
