Reaching into a past filled with glories and terror, the Exalt shapes a divinely-inspired order for her army to follow. Whenever the Exalt uses [[Battle Path Ascendant]], she may use this Charm to create and employ a stratagem with a threshold of half the successes of her [[Join Battle]] roll, rounded up. This stratagem must make sense in the context of the battlefield.
