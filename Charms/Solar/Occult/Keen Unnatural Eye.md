*Immersion into the occult opens the Lawgiver’s eyes to evidence of spirit actions.*
She more readily and easily sees the handiwork of ghosts and demons, and can more skillfully detect the footsteps of spirits. When using [[Survival]] or [[Investigation]] to track a spirit or uncover evidence of its actions, the Exalt gains ([[Essence]] or three, whichever is greater) bonus dice to the attempt.
