# Traits
Begin with the defaults: 
- [[Essence]] 1
- [[Willpower]] 5
- ([[Essence]] x 3) + 10 Personal Essence pool.
- ([[Essence]] x 7) + 26 Peripheral Essence pool.
# Concept & Caste
Determine a character concept and select a Caste -  this provides **Caste Abilities** and **anima powers**:
- [[Azimuth]] - Warriors, officers, and generals.
- [[Ascendant]] - Saints, prophets, and exiles.
- [[Horizon]] - Artisans, physicians, and sorcerers. 
- [[Nadir]] - Assassins, outlaws, spies.
- [[Penumbra]] - Politician, socialite, crime boss.
# Attributes
Each attribute starts with 1 dot assigned to it. The attributes are divided in 3 categories:

| Physical      | Social           | Mental           |
| ------------- | ---------------- | ---------------- |
| [[Strength]]  | [[Charisma]]     | [[Perception]]   |
| [[Dexterity]] | [[Manipulation]] | [[Intelligence]] |
| [[Stamina]]   | [[Appearance]]   | [[Wits]]         |
Allocate 1 of the categories as the **primary** category, another as the **secondary** category, and the last as the **tertiary** category.
- Distribute 8 dots among the **primary** attributes.
- Distribute 6 dots among the **secondary** attributes.
- Distribute 4 dots among the **tertiary** attributes.

Attributes cannot be raised above 5.
# Abilities
Each Caste has 8 **Caste Abilities** associated with it (ctrl + hover to preview):
- [[Azimuth#Caste Abilities| Azimuth Caste Abilities]]
- [[Ascendant#Caste Abilities|Ascendant Caste Abilities]]
- [[Horizon#Caste Abilities|Horizon Caste Abilities]]
- [[Nadir#Caste Abilities|Nadir Caste Abilities]]
- [[Penumbra#Caste Abilities|Penumbra Caste Abilities]]

Select 5 of the 8 **Caste Abilities** associated with your selected Caste, these will be the **Caste Abilities** for your character.
Additionally, select 5 **Favoured Abilities**. You cannot select abilities you have chosen as your **Caste Abilities**.

From your chosen **Caste Abilities** select 1 to be your [[Primordial Ability]].

Divide 28 dots among your abilities.
Each ability can have a maximum of 3 dots allocated to it (before bonus points are applied).
Each **Favoured Ability** must have at least 1 dot allocated to it.

Assign 4 [[Specialty|Specialties]].
# Merits
Assign 10 dots to [[Merits]].
- [[Merits#Innate|Innate Merits]] may only be purchased now.
- [[Merits#Story|Story Merits]] may be purchased now, or with Storyteller permission.
- [[Merits#Purchased|Purchased Merits]] may be purchased at any time.

Some [[Merits]] can make different options for charms available:
- [[Artifact]] may allow Evocations.
- [[Martial Artist]] allows Martial Arts Styles and Charms.

Many Infernals have mutations reflecting the unwoven coadjutor they merged with, including [[Merits#Supernatural|Supernatural Merits]].

Infernals who venture to the Demon City and embrace their role as Hell’s Green Sun Princes
distribute five additional dots among the following [[Merits]]:
- [[Backing]]
- [[Influence]]
- [[Manse]]
- [[Resources]]
- [[Retainers]]

All Exalted gain [[Exalted Healing]] for free.

Optionally take [[Flaws]] for additional experience triggers during play.
# Charms
Select 15 #Infernal Charms for which you will meet the minimums and prerequisite at the end of character creation.
Treat your Essence as 5 for meeting the prerequisites of charms in your [[Primordial Ability]].

Instead of purchasing Charms, you are able to purchase [[Evocations]] and [[Martial Arts]], or spells for sorcery or necromancy if an appropriate initiation Charm has been purchased.
# Devil-Body
Create your [[Devil-Body]], a unique transformation that can be monstrous and demonic.

Select 5 dots of [[Merits#Mutations|mutations]] and 5 dots of [[Devil-Body]] Powers.

The [[Devil-Body]] can only be entered once per story, so this does not need to be created at character creation.
# Intimacies
Create at least four [[Intimacy|Intimacies]]. 
- At least one must be Defining.
- At least one must be Major.
- At least one must be negative.
- At least one must be positive.

Many Infernals have Principles or Ties related to those who wronged them and how they seek to obtain vengeance or justice. Other common Intimacies include Ties to specific demons or Yozis.

There’s no maximum on how many Intimacies you may choose.
# Bonus Points
Spend 15 bonus points according to the following table at any point during the character creation process:

| Trait                                  | Bonus Point Cost |
| -------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| Primary or Secondary Attribute         | 4 per dot        |
| Tertiary Attribute                     | 3 per dot        |
| Caste or Favoured Ability              | 1 per dot        |
| Other Ability                          | 2 per dot        |
| Specialty                              | 1 each           |
| Merits                                 | 1 per dot        |
| Caste or Favoured Charm                | 4 each           |
| Other Charm                            | 5 each           |
| Spell (Occult Caste or Favoured)       | 4 each           |
| Spell (Occult non-Caste, non-Favoured) | 5 each           |
| Evocation                              | 4 each           |
| Willpower                              | 2 per dot        |
# Final checks

- **Favoured Abilities** must have at least one dot at the end of character creation.
- Abilities and Attributes may never be raised above 5. 
- You may not have a *Specialty* in an ability rated 0 dots.
