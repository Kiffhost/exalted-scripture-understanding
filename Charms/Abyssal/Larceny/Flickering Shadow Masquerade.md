*The Abyssal shrouds herself in falsehoods with but a gesture, changing faces between blinks of an eye.*
The Abyssal completes a disguise roll in seconds.
She can ignore penalties for lacking appropriate equipment with clever stunting. In combat, she gains (Manipulation) Initiative if a successful disguise roll provides a tactical advantage.
With a Larceny 5, Essence 3 repurchase, the Abyssal can pay a five-mote, one-Willpower surcharge to use this Charm reflexively. In combat, she can only use it on her turn. This makes it compatible with Simple Charms like False Heart Mien.
