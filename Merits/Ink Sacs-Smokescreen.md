The mutant can blind pursuers with ink sacs, an acrid spray, or similar diversions. Once per scene, when she makes a disengage roll, she can release this diversion. If successful, each enemy that opposed her is blinded (Exalted, p. 168) until he clears his eyes as a miscellaneous action.
