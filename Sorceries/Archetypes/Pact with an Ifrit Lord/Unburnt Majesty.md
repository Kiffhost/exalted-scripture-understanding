Having contracted with a lord of flame, the sorcerer commands the fealty of lesser fires. She doubles 8s on rolls to resist [[environmental hazards]] based on fire or heat, and adds her [[Essence]] to both [[soak]] and [[hardness]] against purely flame-based attacks such as [[firewand]] blasts.
