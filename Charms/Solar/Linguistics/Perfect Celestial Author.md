While using Flashing Quill Atemi, the Lawgiver can edit a manuscript to combine Simple-type Charms or effects which could otherwise not coexist. Thus, she could use Letter-Within-A-Letter Technique and Cup Boils Over to kill someone with what appears to be a love letter.
