*Striking with overwhelming force, the Heaven and Earth Gauntlets clear the battlefield of foes.*
This Evocation waives the [[Initiative]] cost and [[Defense]] penalty for making a smash attack (Exalted, p. 586). The wielder adds ([[Strength]]) bonus dice to a withering smash attack’s raw damage, or doubles 10s on a decisive smash attack’s damage roll.
