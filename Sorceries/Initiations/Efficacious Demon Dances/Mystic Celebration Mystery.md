As long as the sorcerer has made a dance-based Performance roll this scene, she may pass of Shape Sorcery actions as mere dance. Characters can see the truth with a read intentions roll, potentially using Performance instead of Socialize, but suffer a three-success penalty. The same penalty applies on any other rolls the Storyteller allows to discover the nature of her dance.
