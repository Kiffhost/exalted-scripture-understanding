*The Abyssal’s anima shrouds her in radiant darkness as the taint of necromancy seeps through it.*
While shaping spells, the Abyssal doesn’t lose necromantic motes for going a turn without gathering them.
At glowing anima or higher, taking grants +1 Defense and +(Anima) soak until her next turn.
