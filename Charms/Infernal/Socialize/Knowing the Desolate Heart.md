*The Infernal understands failure, whether the petty follies of god and man or the crushing defeat of the Yozis.*

The Infernal makes a special read intentions roll against a character. Success reveals whatever he believes the greatest failure in his life has been. It also reveals a relevant Intimacy, if he has one.
## [[Keys]]
#### Inner Voice Betrayal
Success lets the Infernal’s player ask the target’s player a question about his failure, or about an Intimacy she’s discovered. Every three extra successes let her ask an additional question.
