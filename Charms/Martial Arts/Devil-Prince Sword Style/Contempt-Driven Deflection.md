*The Devil-Prince’s weapon swings wide, disdain for her attackers eating into their confidence and causing fatal pauses and errors in their attacks.*

The stylist gains +1 Parry against an attack. If her attacker has a [[Devil-Prince Sword Style#Craven Intimacies|craven Intimacy]], the attack roll suffers a −(Intimacy) penalty.

If the stylist successful defends, her victim suffers −1 Resolve against her instill and threaten rolls until the end of her next turn.
