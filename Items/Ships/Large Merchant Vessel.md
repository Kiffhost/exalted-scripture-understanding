What these wallowing ships lack in speed and agility, they make up for in sheer bulk transport capability. Such prize ships are often accompanied by more nimble protective vessels; alternately, they may transport armies abroad, or act as floating artillery platforms.
# Role
Heavy seagoing merchant vessel; large troop carrier.
# Cargo
3,000 tons or more. Troop transports may carry between 250-500 troops, depending on the length of the voyage.
