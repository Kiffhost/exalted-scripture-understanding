The hoofbeats of the Dragon-Blood’s mount strike the ground as swiftly and lightly as pearls falling from a broken necklace, speeding her onwards to her goal. She may add automatic successes to a Ride roll for two motes each, and she ignores one point of mobility penalty from her mount’s barding.
