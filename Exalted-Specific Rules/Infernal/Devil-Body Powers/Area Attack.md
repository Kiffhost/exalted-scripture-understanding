The Infernal can attack several enemies at once, either because she possesses a vast body, many swarming limbs, or can unleash devastating supernatural forces like a bone-breaking harmony sung by many mouths. She chooses one of her natural attacks or an attack from the [[Enhanced Attack]] feature. She can use it to make attacks against all enemies within close range of a point within its range:
- On withering attacks, only the highest damage roll awards Initiative.
- On decisive attacks, she divides her Initiative evenly among all hit enemies, rounding up, to determine the damage rolled against them.
- On gambits, she divides her Initiative evenly among hit enemies, rounding up, for Initiative rolls. She only needs to pay the gambitâ€™s Initiative cost once.

Area attacks are incompatible with Simple Charms.

An alternative version of this power lets the Infernal roll her full Initiative against each enemy hit by a decisive attack or gambit or gain the Initiative from all enemies hit by a withering attack. However, she can only do so once per scene. She can purchase both versions separately.
