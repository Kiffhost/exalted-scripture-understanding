*The deathknight swings huge, foe-cleaving blades with effortless prowess, striking with control born of unloving strength.*
The Abyssal can use Strength instead of Dexterity on attacks with heavy and medium weapons, and to calculate her Parry with such weapons. This doesn’t benefit from effects that grant bonus Strength dots, double successes on Strength rolls, or replace her Strength with a higher value.
