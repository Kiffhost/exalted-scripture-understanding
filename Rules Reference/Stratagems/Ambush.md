The victorious general tricks his enemy into a trap, joining battle when it is least expected! All attacks launched by his forces during the first round of combat are considered [[Rules Reference/Combat/Stealth#Ambush|Ambush]] attacks. All attacks launched by his forces in the following 3 rounds are considered [[Rules Reference/Combat/Stealth#Surprise Attack|Surprise Attacks]].
