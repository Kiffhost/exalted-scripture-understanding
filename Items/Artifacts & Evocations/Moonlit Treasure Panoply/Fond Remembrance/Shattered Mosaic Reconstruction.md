Once Fond Remembrance’s victim begins to believe in one memory, he opens his mind to more.
When the wielder makes a false memory gambit against an enemy with an Intimacy related to a false memory, the gambit’s Initiative cost is reduced by (Intimacy).
This includes Intimacies created by Heartfelt Memory Feint, instilled with social influence, formed voluntarily by the target, and so on.
