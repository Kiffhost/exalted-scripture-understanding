*Demons recognize the sorcerer as one favored by Mara.*
First Circle demons are treated as having a Minor [[Intimacy|Tie]] of terrified awe towards her, while Second and Third Circle demons have a positive Minor [[Intimacy|Tie]] of respect, or some variation suited to their nature.
