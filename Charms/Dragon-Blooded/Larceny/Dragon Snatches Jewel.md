*The Dragon-Blood’s grasp is as sure as stone, capable of wresting away the weapons of the Anathema.*
She makes a disarm gambit from close range with ([[Dexterity]] + [[Larceny]]), doubling 9s. On a successful gambit, the Exalt may reflexively ready the disarmed weapon and is refunded the gambit’s [[Initiative]] cost. If she disarms an artifact weapon, she reflexively !!attunestoitatnocostfortherestofthescene.Thisbreaks!! the original character’s attunement. Alternatively, she may steal a hearthstone out of the socket of an enemy’s artifact, likewise stealing attunement to it for the rest of the scene. This Charm can only be used once per scene.
