The sorcerer is immune to the twisting power of the Wyld, and need never fear mutation, delusion, or addiction from time spent in chaos. However, her Wyld affinity makes her vulnerable to iron, causing her to take aggravated damage from weapons made of it as though she were one of the raksha.
