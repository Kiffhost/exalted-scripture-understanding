*Fate conspires in the Sidereal’s strategy, submitting to the mounting pressure of her inevitable victory on the weave of events.*
To use this Charm, the Sidereal must be fighting under a stratagem or prophecy she’s enacted. An allied battle group gains −1 target number on all rolls.
This Charm can be stacked to benefit multiple battle groups.
