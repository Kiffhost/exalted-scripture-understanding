*Spotting a fateful opportunity, the Sidereal looses a wellplaced arrow.*
The Sidereal adds (higher of Perception or Wits) to her Initiative to determine when she acts, but she must use her turn to attack. If she attacks an enemy who hasn’t acted that round, her damage roll gains −1 target number.
