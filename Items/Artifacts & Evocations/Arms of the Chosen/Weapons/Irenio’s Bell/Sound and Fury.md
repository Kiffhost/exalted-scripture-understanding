The Exalt’s affinity with her shield has grown, guiding her movements in time with its music. Whenever she gains Vibration from blocking an attack, she gains one Initiative. 

## Dissonant

Characters dissonant with orichalcum cannot awaken this Evocation. 

## Resonant

If this Evocation brings the wielder out of Initiative Crash by raising her Initiative above 0, she reflexively rolls Join Battle with (Charisma + Performance).
