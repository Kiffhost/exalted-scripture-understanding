*Opulence and hedonism nourish the sorcerer's soul.*
Once per scene, when she partakes in a gala, festival, night of drunken carousing, or other form of revelry, she rolls ([[Charisma]] + [[Occult]]) against difficulty 3, adding non-Charm dice equal to the total [[Resources]] cost of the event, gaining sorcerous motes equal to (1 + extra successes), which last until the end of the day.
