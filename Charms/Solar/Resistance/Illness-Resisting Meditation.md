*The Lawgiver’s immune system is fortified by inexorable spirit energies that purge her body of malaise.*
This Charm adds ([[Essence]] +1) automatic successes to the Exalt’s attempt to resist a disease’s virulence, as well as its morbidity. If the Exalt wishes to get sick, she can choose to apply this Charm’s effects solely to the morbidity roll.
