The sorcerer grasps reality by confronting ignorance and illusion. Once per scene, when she [[Introducing a Fact|introduces]] or [[Challenging a Fact|challenges a fact]] or successfully asserts her [[Resolve]], she gains ([[Perception]] + [[stunt]] rating) [[Rules Reference/Sorcery/Sorcery#Motes|sorcerous motes]], which last until the scene ends.
