*Huskflayer gnaws greedily at its master’s flesh, sating itself on a willing offering.*
The wielder gains one gore point, plus additional points equal to the wound penalty of the health level damaged by this Evocation’s costs — e.g., damaging a −2 level would yield three points. Damaging temporary health levels only awards one point, regardless of their wound level.
## Reset 
Once per day. With [[Essence]] 3, resonant wielders can use this Evocation once per scene.
