*The Lawgiver embodies virility, magnetism and grace, empowering her forces of persuasion.*
For five motes, this Charm grants three bonus dice to all forms of social influence from all Abilities except [[Stealth]]. In addition, the cost of all social influence [[Charms]] are reduced by one mote, to a minimum of one. The seven mote version of this Charm extends the duration to indefinite.
