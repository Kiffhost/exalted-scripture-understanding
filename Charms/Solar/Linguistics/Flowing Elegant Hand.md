*Drawing on the [[Essence]] of her formidable mind, the Exalt perfects her arguments and sharpens her words, so that her writing is pristine, flawless and precise.*
This Charm supplements written [[Linguistics]] actions with double 9s. At [[Linguistics]] 5+, [[Essence]] 3+, she may repurchase this Charm, learning a three mote version that doubles 8s. This repurchase requires that she know [[Flawless Brush Discipline]]. At [[Linguistics]] 5+, [[Essence]] 5+, she may purchase this Charm a third time, learning a four mote version that grants double 7s.
