*Creation’s destiny is the Sidereal’s greatest masterpiece; sustaining the cosmic order renews her creative energies.*
The Sidereal gains the following benefits: 
- She adds ([[Craft]]) dice on [[Lore]] and [[Occult]] rolls involving knowledge of fate, destiny, and enemies of fate. 
- Fulfilling an auspicious prospect or using an Ascending (Caste) Horoscope or Descending (Caste) Horoscope Charm awards craft points as per a basic project (Exalted, p. 240). 
- Successfully enacting a prophecy awards gold points equal to its highest Ambition Factor. 
- She can pay the experience point cost of prophecies with white points, paying five white points per experience point.
