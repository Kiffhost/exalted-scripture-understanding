Sight of the Lawgiver’s ship strikes dread into the heart of her enemies. Its sudden disappearance is a presage to disaster. This Charm supplements a concealment action in which the Exalt’s ship quickly vanishes behind a piece of scenery, adding the ship’s speed as bonus dice to the roll.
