The movements of Seven Star Alignment’s sections reveal Pavonis’ sorcerous battle-stance to its wielder. Upon entering a Martial Arts Form that’s compatible with seven-section staffs, the wielder may reflexively make a shape sorcery roll to begin or continue casting a spell. 
## Reset
Once per round.
