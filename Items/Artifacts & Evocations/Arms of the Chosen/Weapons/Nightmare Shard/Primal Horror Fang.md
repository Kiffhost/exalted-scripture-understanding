*Nightmare Shard moves like no thing of the world should, leaving wounds that bleed with the oily black ichor of spilt dreams.*
The [[skycutter]] deals aggravated damage to enemies afflicted with cracked mirror blight, and adds (Intensity) bonus dice on withering damage rolls against them. Creatures of the Wyld count as having it at Defining intensity. 

## Resonant

The damage bonus from this Evocation applies on decisive attacks as well.
