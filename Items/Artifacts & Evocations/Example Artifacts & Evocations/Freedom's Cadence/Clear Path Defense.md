The more time a single Exalt spends with Freedom’s Cadence, the more accustomed the armor becomes to the obstacles that stand in her way, and thus accustomed to nudging her out of alignment with them. When the Exalt awakens this Evocation, the armor gains a permanent +1 increase to its soak.
