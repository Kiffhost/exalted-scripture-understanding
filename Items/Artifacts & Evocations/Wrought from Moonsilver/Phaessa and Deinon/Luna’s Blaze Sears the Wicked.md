The Lunar rouses the moon-gods slumbering within the blades. This Evocation upgrades the following Charms: 
- Claw That Rends the Veil (p. XX) can enhance the blades. 
- While the blades are waxing, she may flurry Argent Guardian Yantra (p. XX) with attacks, ignoring flurry penalties. 
- Demon-Drinking Fang’s mote cost (p. XX) is waived while the blades are waning.
