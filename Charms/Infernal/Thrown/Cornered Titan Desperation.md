*There is no cowardice In the face of necessity.*

The Infernal adds one non-Charm die on a decisive attack roll for each condition that applies. If she meets at least two conditions, she adds her attack roll extra successes as dice of damage.
- The attack is unexpected.
- Her target is at close range.
- Her target is crashed.
- Her target is actively restraining her or blocking the only exit from a confined space.
