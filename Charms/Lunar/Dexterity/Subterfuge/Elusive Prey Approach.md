*Adapting to the flow of an enemy’s attack, the Lunar moves with it to step into her attacker’s blind spot.*
After dodging an attack, she may use this Charm to reflexively roll to enter concealment, subject to the usual penalties to [[Traits/Abilities/Stealth]] in combat.
