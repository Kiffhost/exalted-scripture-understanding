*Soldiers see fire on the horizon and know that their general stands with them, [[Sunset Blaze]] in hand.*
Its wielder adds (higher of [[Essence]] or 3) dice on a rally or [[rally for numbers]] action, and gains [[Initiative]] equal to the 10s on her roll.

## Special activation rules
A Dragon-Blooded Cathak scion awakens this Evocation at no cost upon attuning to [[Sunset Blaze]].
