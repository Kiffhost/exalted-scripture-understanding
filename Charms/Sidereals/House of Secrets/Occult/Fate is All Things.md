Those untethered from fate’s weave are still bound by the iron web of the Sidereal’s will. The Sidereal treats un-Exalted enemies of fate as spirits for purposes of Sidereal Charms. This doesn’t apply for Charms that affect specific types of spirit, like gods, only those applicable to all spirits.
