The Lunar’s all-encompassing understanding of her forces ensures their deployment makes the most of their strengths. She adds a success to a Strategic Maneuver roll, and ignores penalties equal to the highest (Might + 1) of any allied battle group. 
## Totemic 

A Lunar whose spirit shape is a creature that hunts in groups may learn this as a Stamina Charm.
