*With words as sharp as needles, Tamokhet’s master confirms her victims’ worst fears.*
When the wielder makes an influence roll that’s opposed by an infected [[Intimacy|Tie]], the Doom penalizes her victim’s [[Resolve]] according to the [[Intimacy|Tie]]’s intensity. 
## Resonant
The infected [[Intimacy]] can’t be used against the wielder’s influence.
