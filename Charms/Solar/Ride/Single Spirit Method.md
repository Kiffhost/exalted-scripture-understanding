The Lawgiver and her mount are as a single being, nearly indivisible. Should her mount lose its footing, the Solar may use this Charm to allow it to reflexively rise from prone. The Solar will neither be thrown from the saddle in this instance, nor will she be injured by her mount rolling on her.
