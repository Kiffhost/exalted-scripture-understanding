*Ajelin’s tarnished moonsilver spreads across its wielder’s skin, encasing her in accursed armor.*
The wielder gains +1 [[Defense|Parry]] and (Enmity) armored soak.
Piercing attacks and similar mundane effects can’t reduce or bypass this soak.
This Evocation’s [[Willpower]] cost is waived while [[Accursed Blade Embodiment]] is active.
## Resonant
This Evocation’s soak can’t be reduced or bypassed by magic.
