*Salty Dog Method Sensing a fault in her actions, the Solar’s indefatigable spirit sets a new course of action.*
With this Charm, the Exalt can reroll any [[Sail]]-based action, keeping successes and rerolling non-successes, activating additional [[Charms]] on the reroll if she chooses to do so.
