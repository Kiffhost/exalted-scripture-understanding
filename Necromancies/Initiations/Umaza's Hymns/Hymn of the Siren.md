*Once per scene, when the necromancer successfully [[Social Influence|influences]] a target to follow her, side with her, or gain or strengthen an [[Intimacy]] towards her, she rolls ([[Manipulation]] + [[Performance]]), adding bonus dice equal to the difference between her [[Appearance]] and his if applicable.*
Each success generates one necromantic mote, which remains until the end of the scene.
