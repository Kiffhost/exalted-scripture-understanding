*Suspending herself in a basin or body of salt water, the sorcerer draws power from the moment she stood on the verge of death.*
After an hour of submerged meditation, she rolls ([[Wits]] + [[Occult]]), gaining [[Rules Reference/Sorcery/Sorcery#Motes|sorcerous motes]] equal to the successes. These last until she performs this ritual again, or until the end of the story. She cannot perform this ritual more than once per day.
