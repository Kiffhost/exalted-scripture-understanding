Fond Remembrance leaves memories that last long after the physical wounds are healed.
Resisting false memories becomes more difficult for characters with [[Intimacy|Intimacies]] related to them. Resisting with [[Willpower]] when they encounter evidence requires a [[Decision Points|Decision Point]]. They must cite an [[Intimacy]] related to the evidence, which must equal or exceed the intensity of the memory’s [[Intimacy]].
