The wielder swathes herself in Phaessa’s magnificent brilliance or Deinon’s dark flames. While the blades are waxing, every 10 on an Appearance-based command action or influence roll rerolls a failed die. While the blades are waning, she rerolls 1s on Stealth rolls until they cease to appear.
