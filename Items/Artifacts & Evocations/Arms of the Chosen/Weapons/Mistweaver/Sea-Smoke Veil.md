The Exalt’s outline blurs and swirls like the mist around her. While she’s in watery concealment, opponents lose one success on attack rolls against her and on [[Awareness]] rolls to penetrate her stealth. 

## Resonant

Unexpected attacks made from watery concealment add one bonus success on damage.
