*As Nyraxes once called a king’s people to a new home, so too may it entrance those who behold its beauty.*
When the lantern-bearer makes a persuade roll to convince others to follow her somewhere, she adds (higher of [[Essence]] or 3) dice. She treats the undead as having a Minor [[Intimacy]] that supports this influence, and can influence even mindless undead.
## Resonant
While Nyraxes’ flame is at its brightest, the lantern-bearer’s influence treats all characters who can see the light as having a Minor [[Intimacy]].
