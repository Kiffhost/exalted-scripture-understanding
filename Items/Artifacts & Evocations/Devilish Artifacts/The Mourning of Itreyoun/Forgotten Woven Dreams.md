*“In time, Itreyoun and its legacy will slip away.”*

The wearer inflicts a −1 penalty on an attack against or a movement action opposing her. The penalty increases to −2 against enemies whose Resolve the wearer has beaten with an inspire roll this scene or who have an Obsession inflicted by this artifact’s Evocations.
### Dissonant
The wearer can only use this Evocation against enemies whose Resolve she’s beaten or who have an Obsession.
### Resonant
The wearer waives the Initiative cost against enemies with lower Initiative.
