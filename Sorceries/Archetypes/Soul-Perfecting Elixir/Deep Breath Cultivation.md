*The humors governed by the element of air reside in the lungs, nourishing the entire body with vital energies.*
As long as the sorcerer abides by an ascetic lifestyle, she may completely refill her [[Essence]] pools with an hour of meditation, and adds her [[Stamina]] to the number of motes she recovers each hour while active.
