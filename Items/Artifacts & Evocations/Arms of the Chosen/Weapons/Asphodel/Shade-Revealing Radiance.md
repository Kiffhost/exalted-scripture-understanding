A black incandescence spills from Asphodel, wreathing the spirits of the dead in a wash of chiaroscuro. For the rest of the scene, immaterial undead within short range are both visible and tangible. All undead thus illuminated glow from within, their secret selves limned in fire that shines through the most dispassionate visage, imposing −1 Guile. 

## Dissonant

This Evocation’s range is restricted to close.
