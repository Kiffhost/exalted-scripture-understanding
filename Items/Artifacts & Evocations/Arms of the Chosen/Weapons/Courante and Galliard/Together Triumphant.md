*When one sword-sibling gains command of the battle, the other is ever ready to share that advantage.*
The wielder gains two [[Initiative]] whenever her sword-sibling gains an [[Initiative]] Break bonus from crashing an enemy. She can only gain this bonus once per round. 

## Dissonant

A character dissonant with orichalcum or moonsilver, as appropriate, cannot awaken this Evocation.
