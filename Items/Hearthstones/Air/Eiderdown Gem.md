*This striated gray-and-white stone feels as light as a soap bubble.*
When socketed into an attuned artifact, it softens gravity’s grip on the bearer. She adds one bonus die to all movement pools when flying or gliding. When she falls from a height, calculate falling damage as though she’d fallen one less range band.
