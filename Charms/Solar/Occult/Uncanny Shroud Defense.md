*On the verge of death, the Exalt draws uncanny [[Essence]] around her like a shroud, stepping between worlds.*
When struck with any kind of damage or attack that would remove her last health level, the Exalt may use this Charm to stop the final level of damage. This Charm cannot be used if the Solar is currently on her last uninjured health level.
