Flying Silver Dream gains the following additional abilities when fighting independently: 
- It may spend one Willpower to use Solar Counterattack (Exalted, p. 347) when an enemy attacks a character it is protecting with a defend other action. 
- It may spend one Willpower to use Unassailable Guardian Posture (Exalted, p. 349). 
- It permanently gains the benefits of Calm and Ready Focus (Exalted, p. 349).
