This stone’s surface is a tangled interweaving of black and white. Socketed in an attuned artifact, it allows bearer and steed to fight as one. Increase the bearer’s mounted combat withering bonus (Exalted, p. 203) by one and increase the difficulty of unhorse gambits against her by one.
