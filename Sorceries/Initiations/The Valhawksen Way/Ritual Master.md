### Ritual Master
Once per week, the sorcerer can add ([[Occult]]) Charm dice on a roll to successfully perform a complex religious or social ritual, a roll based on strategy — including [[Strategic Warfare|Strategic Maneuver]] rolls and rolls to play Gateway or other games of strategy — or a roll based on meditation, often made as part of a Charm.
