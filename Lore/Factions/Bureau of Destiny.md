The part of the [[Celestial Bureaucracy]] to which the Sidereals belong, and from which they influence both [[Yu-Shan|Heaven]] and [[Creation]]'s future.
Aside from the complexities of the official structure of the [[Celestial Bureaucracy]], the Sidereals are truly split between the two political blocs of the [[Bronze Faction]] and the [[Gold Faction]].
