The Sidereal’s performance puts her audience at ease, freeing them to make choices unfettered by fear or worry. When the Sidereal makes a persuade roll, it also counts a roll to inspire calm, hope, or happiness. Her targets can’t use [[Intimacy|Intimacies]] of fear, doubt, or insecurity to bolster their [[Resolve]] or in Decision Points; instead, they support the Sidereal’s influence.
