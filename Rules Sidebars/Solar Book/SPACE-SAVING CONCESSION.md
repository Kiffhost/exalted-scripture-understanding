Exalted 3rd p.270

DEVELOPER 5, ESSENCE 3 
 
Multiple Charms are considered to have the following special activation rules: When a Solar is confronted with multiple concealed enemies, her senses are further heightened. The Awareness Excellency and each Reflexive Awareness Charms with an instant duration
```query
path:Charms/Solar/Awareness  ["type":reflexive] ["duration":Instant] -file:(Blink Roused Dragon Detection)
```
— except for [[Blink]] and [[Roused Dragon Detection]]—can have their durations extended to one turn by paying an extra two motes upon activation. If such Charms are combined, a single two mote surcharge extends all of them.
