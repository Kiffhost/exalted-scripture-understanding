*A vermilion line bisects this gray-brown gemstone.*
Socketed in an attuned artifact, it magnifies the bearer’s knack for destruction, adding one bonus die to feats of destruction. If it’s set in a weapon that’s used to stunt a feat of demolition, the bonus die becomes an automatic success.
