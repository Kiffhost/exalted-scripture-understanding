**Example Manse**: A distant temple of hand-stacked stones worn smooth by time and tears. 
This uncut sapphire glows from within with a dim light. The bearer adds one non-Charm success on [[read intentions]] rolls to discover [[Intimacy|Intimacies]] rooted in sorrow, or regret, as well as to [[Instill]] rolls to strengthen such [[Intimacy|Intimacies]].
